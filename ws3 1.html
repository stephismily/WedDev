ABOUT ME

But before that, let me introduce myself properly!

As you might have already read, my name is Stephina Smily. I am an MCA student at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore. I completed my BSc in Computer Science from PSG College of Arts & Science, Coimbatore. But back in Grade XII, I was a Maths-Biology student. Doesn’t make much sense, right? I know — I was confused too in the beginning. Coming from a non-core background, when I first entered my UG days, I hardly knew anything beyond switching on the system and the basic computer stuff everyone is familiar with (I wasn’t that dumb though!).

But then I had to learn everything from scratch. And I did. I learn't Python, DSA, SQL (my most fav!) and many such things. And here came the tricky part - I had to identify which domain I was actually interested in. However, I din't have any clue about the domains that existed. That din't stop me still. I did my reasearch. Whenever something new was mentioned in the class, I'd google it.

That's when I came across Data. It was everywhere. Even the most mundane thing that we come across in our day-to-day life could be measured, defined and represented as data. And I wanted to work with it. One way of doing it was Analysis. And suddenly I wanted to be Data Analyst. Almost all the requirements of being a data analyst pulled me in. Working with Excel, I loved it - Learning SQL, I'd die for it - asking questions, what else have I been doing all my life! I decided for myself, one day I'd become a senior Data Analyst in a leading FinTech corporation.

In that process, I have been continuously upskilling myself in the tools that analysts regularly use - Excel, MySQL, PowerBI etc. I have been also practicing case studies and taking up certifications that aid me in the process. And quite frankly, I'm enjoying the process. I have not reached the goal point yet - not even close - but I'm getting there, step-by-step and I'll try my level best to make it.
